    Post Campaign Report

    Summer 2016

    Summary

    "The SWISS campaign threw numerous challenges at the team but it was great to see it come together. The end result was a stunning looking microsite and moreover it produced some really impressive results!

    Bringing together a number of key Swiss experts allowed us to produce some genuinely credible and useful content and it showed with over 25% of visits coming to the site through natural traffic via the SWISS tab in the Secret Escapes navigation.

    We saw over 13,808 entrants to the competition, 261,471 video views on Facebook and a record breaking 112 sales for a single Switzerland package (sourced exclusively for this campaign this generated over £85k in revenue in a single week) - it seems that Secret Escape members genuinely love SWISS and Switzerland...."

    Jess Little - Deputy UK Editor

    Response

    In order to fulfill the campaign Secret Escapes...

    1. Built a fully integrated SWISS Summer section into the Secret Escapes site
    2. Sourced 3 Swiss experts (advocates) from the world of photography, adventure sports and social media to help create credible and exclusive content
    3. Showcased hiking, biking and relaxation through interactive 360 VR video content
    4. Secured exclusive packages including SWISS flights, offering £25 off selected SWISS breaks
    5. Launched an extensive Social campaign across Facebook, Twitter and Instagram to showcase 360 video, imagery and promote the competition
    6. Created 4 dedicated solus emails, targeting our members by previous behaviour (speaking to members who have booked or browsed offers to Switzerland or competing destinations)
    7. Developed a competition mechanic to capture all members interested in travelling to Switzerland

    Highlights

    1. The site had 84,082 unique views
    2. The competition generated 13,808 entrants with 5,713 of them opting in to SWISS re-marketing
    3. Secret Escapes sold a record amount of Switzerland Package sales (112 including SWISS flights) on one offer (exclusively sourced with Inghams for the campaign)
    4. The videos generated over 260k views on Facebook alone and 1,617 interactions and reactions
    5. We sent 2,090 clicks from the hub through to SWISS booking pages
    6. The HPTO generated a 1.71% CTR and the best performing creative was the city image used
    7. The competition email generated a 3.18% CTR and 12.68% CTOR

    Learnings

    1. Static 360 images are more impactful on desktop
    2. Mobile is a more effective way to showcase 360 video
    3. Secret Escapes members are more receptive to SWISS packages compared to flight and hotel only
    4. An additional week needs to be factored in for testing across devices
    5. A competition is a hugely effective way of gathering data

    ROS Display Advertising

    Driving to the Swiss Summer Hub

    With the ROS of site display we targeted members based in London and the South East.

    Swiss imagery lends itself to landscape display, thus the billboard and native 678x228 formats saw the best click through rates across display

    The Billboard saw a brilliant 0.8% click through rate.

    Mid campaign we designed two more HPTO creatives in order to optimise the HPTO and keep the creative fresh, this was beneficial as the final city creative had the best click through rate of 2.01%
